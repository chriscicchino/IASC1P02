ABOUT


Niagara Store Fixtures Inc. takes great pride in all aspects of its work and the quality of the fixtures produced speaks for itself. With over 30 years of experience manufacturing store fixtures and millwork throughout North America, our staff is experienced in a vast variety of projects and materials. Our field of expertise ranges from projects in the jewellery showcase, retail store, financial institution and salon industries.


Utilizing technology and skilled craftspeople we are able to produce the most intricate and demanding designs. Having vast experience working with architectural materials such as wood, laminates, metal, plastic, marble & stone, solid surface and lighting, we are able to produce a wide spectrum of quality and unique pieces.


At Niagara Store Fixtures Inc. we are able to work from supplied drawings or design our own projects utilizing computer aided design. Working from a simple sketch or architectural drawings, we are able to turn the design into reality.


Strategic insight, exceptional design, impeccable craftsmanship, and commitment to managing every step of the process. Having built thousands of retail environments for hundreds of North America’s largest and smallest brands, we know what it takes to grow a retail business from their 3rd location to their 4th, or from their 10th to their 20th, or even from their 100th to their 1000th.
